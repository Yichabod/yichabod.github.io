Assorted resources for chronic pain

(Last updated March 11th, 2023)

This is a scattered list of the resources I have either read or plan to look more into regarding chronic pain. I have been sitting on this for too long so am releasing this disorganized collection on the hopes it is more helpful for people than nothing. I will have better documentation in the future.

Books

Healing Back Pain, John Sarno

The Mindbody Syndrome, John Sarno

A Guide to Better Movement, Hargrove

  • Has a fairly detailed biological account of chronic pain

Unlearn Your Pain, Schubiner

Plague Time, Ewald

  • Examining link between chronic pain and viruses. Probably not useful if you as an individual already have chronic pain

After Phrenology, Anderson

The Mindbody Syndrome (TMS): A Path to Recovery & Freedom, Viner

(Uncertain)

The Pain Management Workbook

Conquering Carpal Tunnel (I think this is wrong but fascinating)

The Body Keeps the Score
